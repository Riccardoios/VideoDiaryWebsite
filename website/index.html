How Video Diary works

Three steps. No onboarding maze, no AI assistant asking how you feel — a video journal you'll actually keep.

Open & record

Launch the app and you're already at the camera. Tap the red REC button, say what's on your mind — a daily prompt helps if you're stuck. Add a mood and an optional note.

Relive & reflect

Every entry lands on your calendar, so scrolling back through your months feels like flipping a diary. The Insight view reveals your mood patterns over time.

Create your montage

When you want the highlight reel, tap Create Montage. Your daily videos become one beautiful movie of memories — no editing required.

Frequently asked questions

What is a video diary and how does it work?

A video diary is a journal you keep by recording short videos instead of writing. You open the app, tap record, speak or film your moment, and the entry is saved to a private calendar you can revisit anytime.

Learn more: how to start a video diary →
What are the benefits of video journaling compared to writing?

Video captures your voice, your face, and the world around you — details text can't hold. It's faster than writing, easier to stick with, and rewatching old entries brings memories back vividly.

Learn more: benefits of video journaling →
What is the best video diary app for iPhone?

The best app is the one you'll open every day. Video Diary is built for that: one-tap recording, a calendar of entries, mood tracking, and a montage maker — no AI, no feed, no account required.

Learn more: choosing a video diary app →
Can I record one second every day and turn it into a movie?

Yes. Record a short clip each day — one second or one minute — and the montage maker combines your daily videos into a single movie of your memories.

Learn more: one second a day journaling →
What should I talk about in my video diary?

Anything on your mind: how the day went, something you're grateful for, a decision you're weighing. Video Diary shows a fresh reflection prompt on the record screen every day, so you never face a blank camera.

Learn more: 30 video journal prompts →
Is my video diary private? Does Video Diary use AI?

Your videos stay yours. There is no AI processing, no hidden sharing, and no social feed — and you can lock the app with Face ID. Private by design.

Learn more: keeping a private video diary →
Can video journaling help with mental health and self-reflection?

Talking to the camera works like thinking out loud: it helps process emotions and build self-awareness. Video Diary adds mood tracking and an Insight view so you can notice patterns over time.

Learn more: video journaling for mental health →
How do I make a montage from my daily videos?

Open the Gallery tab and tap Create Montage. Video Diary automatically combines your entries into one beautiful movie — no editing skills needed.

Yes — free to download and free for daily video journaling. An optional Premium subscription adds exclusive color palettes, unique fonts, Face ID lock, and iCloud backup.
